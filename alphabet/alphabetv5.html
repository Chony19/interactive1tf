For this project, I have designed a new typeface, Olfutur. Olfutur is the result of a combination of the words "old" and "future". The design process was inspired by the first project about Time Capsule, hence, the idea of having a pixelated style resembles a bitmap font that was used in old screens with low resolution. I based my inspiration by the game Tetris where I used the shapes that the game offers and from there, constructed the English alphabet, numbers, and punctuation. The interactive part of this project happens when the user hovers or clocks over the black squares so that animation can play.
