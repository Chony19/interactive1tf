This website is created to show how we used to have a completely separate and different device compared to cellphones. With the lack of huge memory storage, we used to carry many game cartridges all at once. I will like to show the early stages of programming before the Internet era and how programmers had to learn a new language.

I decided to base my theme on videogames to show how people used to entertain themselves before the introduction of smartphones.
