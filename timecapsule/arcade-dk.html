Donkey Kong was first released in 1981 as a single-player arcade platform. Released by Nintendo, it was the first video game to feature the jumping technique making other games adopt the technique. Donkey Kong is also one of the earliest examples of platform games. The game consists of maneuvering the main character to reach to the top and rescue the damsel in distress. The main character must move between platforms while dodging objectives that are thrown by Donkey Kong who is next to the damsel.
