Street Fighter was released in 1987 by a Japanese video game developer and publisher, Capcom. Street Fighter is considered as the first competitive fighting game and the inaugural game for its series. The game consists of one-on-one matches against a player or a computer-controlled opponent. The gameplay is structured of three rounds, the player with two wins will become the winner of the match. The controllers are more sophisticated compared to the other games. Having a joystick to muce the character sideways and six buttons for actions divided into two categories, punch and kick. There are three different punches: jab, strong, or fierce punch; and three different kicks: short, forward, or roundhouse kick.
