Pac-Man, released in 1980, was developed by Namco a former Japanese developer and publisher of arcade and home console. Its original name was Puck-Man, however, the developers changed the name to Pac-Man for international release. The game consists of a yellow character in which the player has to control. The objective is to control Pac-Man to eat all the white dots inside the maze while escaping from the ghosts.
