The Legend of Zelda: Link’s Awakening was released in 1993 by the developer Nintendo EAD. Unlike other Legend of Zelda games, Link’s Awakening doesn’t feature Princess Zelda nor the Triforce. The game consists of the main character, Link, with a mission to search eight musical instruments that will awaken a certain character that will let him escape the island.
