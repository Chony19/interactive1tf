Space Invaders was developed by Taito in 1978. Taito is a Japanese company that specializes in video games, toys, and game centers. Space Invaders was the first fixed shooter genre where its goal is to defeat waves of descending aliens. The game consists of a space shuttle that moves horizontally while its automatic shooting lasers fire to defeat the incoming aliens.
